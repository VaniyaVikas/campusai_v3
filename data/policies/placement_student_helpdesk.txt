PLACEMENT AND STUDENT HELPDESK POLICIES
Academic Year 2024-25

Placement Eligibility Policy
-----------------------------
1. Students are eligible for campus placement if they have:
   a) No active backlogs (pending ATKT subjects) at the time of placement drive
   b) A minimum CGPA of 6.0 out of 10
   c) Minimum 75% attendance in the current semester
   d) Completed all required internship credits
2. Students with a single active backlog may participate with written approval from the
   Placement Officer, subject to company discretion.
3. Students who have already accepted an offer from a previous placement drive are not
   eligible to participate in further drives (one offer per student policy).
4. Students must register on the placement portal at least 7 days before the drive.
5. Dress code for placement drives is formal attire. Students in informal dress will
   not be permitted to enter.
6. Students must carry their college ID, updated resume (3 copies), and all original
   certificates for placement drives.

Placement Registration
----------------------
1. Online registration opens 14 days before each placement drive.
2. Registration fee is Rs. 0 (free for all eligible students).
3. Students must update their resume and skill set on the portal before registration.
4. Confirmation email will be sent 3 days before the drive.

Student Helpdesk Policy
-----------------------
1. The Student Helpdesk is available Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 5 PM.
2. Queries can be submitted in-person, via email to helpdesk@college.edu, or through
   this AI system.
3. Response time for non-urgent queries is 2 working days.
4. Urgent queries (exam-related, medical, fee deadline) are addressed within 4 hours.
5. Students may escalate unresolved queries to the Dean of Student Affairs after 5 working days.
6. All helpdesk interactions are logged and tracked by ticket number.
7. Anonymous complaints can be submitted via the suggestion box at the main office.

Grievance Redressal
-------------------
1. Academic grievances must be submitted in writing to the Academic Committee within
   15 days of the event.
2. The committee will respond within 10 working days.
3. Appeals against committee decisions can be made to the Principal within 7 days.
