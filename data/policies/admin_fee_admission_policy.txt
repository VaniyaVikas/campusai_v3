FEE PAYMENT AND ADMISSION POLICIES
Academic Year 2024-25

Fee Payment Policy
------------------
1. Semester fees must be paid before the 15th of the first month of each semester.
2. A late fee of Rs. 50 per day is charged after the due date.
3. Students with pending fees exceeding one semester will not be allowed to appear in examinations.
4. Fee receipts must be kept safely; duplicates are issued at Rs. 50.
5. Fee refund is applicable only if admission is cancelled within 7 days of joining.
6. Scholarship students must submit scholarship approval letter to the accounts department
   before the fee deadline to avoid late fee charges.

Admission Policy
----------------
1. Admission to all undergraduate programs requires a minimum of 45% marks in 12th standard.
2. For Science programs (B.Sc., B.Tech), minimum 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
   is required at 12th standard level.
3. Reserved category students (SC/ST/OBC) get a 5% relaxation in minimum eligibility criteria.
4. Admission forms must be submitted with the following documents:
   a) 10th and 12th marksheets (original + 2 photocopies)
   b) School Leaving Certificate
   c) Caste Certificate (if applicable)
   d) Aadhar Card photocopy
   e) 4 passport size photographs
5. Admission is confirmed only upon payment of the first semester fees.
6. Transfer admissions from other universities require a No-Objection Certificate (NOC)
   from the previous institution.

Attendance Policy
-----------------
1. Minimum 75% attendance is mandatory in each subject per semester.
2. Students with attendance below 60% will be debarred from appearing in semester examinations.
3. Students with attendance between 60%-75% may appeal to the Academic Committee with
   valid medical or other documented reasons.
4. Medical leave of up to 10 days per semester is considered with a valid doctor's certificate.
5. Attendance is calculated separately for theory and practical classes.
