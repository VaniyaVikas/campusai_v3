COLLEGE EXAMINATION RULES AND REGULATIONS
Academic Year 2024-25

ATKT (Allowed To Keep Terms) Policy
------------------------------------
1. A student is eligible to appear in the ATKT examination if they have failed in not more than
   4 subjects in the current semester.
2. Students who have failed in 5 or more subjects are NOT eligible for ATKT and must repeat
   the semester.
3. The ATKT examination form must be submitted within 15 days of the declaration of results.
4. ATKT examination fee is Rs. 500 per subject.
5. A student can avail ATKT benefit for a maximum of 3 consecutive semesters per subject.
6. Students on academic probation are not eligible for ATKT without written approval from the
   Academic Committee.

Hall Ticket Policy
------------------
1. Hall tickets will be issued only to students who have:
   a) Cleared all pending fees
   b) Submitted all required assignments
   c) Maintained minimum 75% attendance
2. Hall tickets must be collected from the examination department at least 3 days before the exam.
3. No hall ticket will be issued on the day of examination.
4. Lost hall tickets can be reissued on payment of Rs. 100 with a written application.

Result and Revaluation
----------------------
1. Students may apply for revaluation within 30 days of result declaration.
2. Revaluation fee is Rs. 300 per subject.
3. Photocopy of answer sheet can be requested at Rs. 200 per subject.
4. Results will not be withheld unless fees are pending.
